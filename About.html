About The FloraNest

The FloraNest (legally registered as Floradesign India Pvt. Ltd.) is a Bangalore-based flower company offering online delivery across the city. We curate splendid products using fresh, exotic blooms for every occasion. An experienced team of florists crafts each bouquet with care and creativity.

Our venture is driven by seasoned flower cultivators who deeply care about the environment. Our flowers are grown on a farm that practices zero-waste and sustainable methods. We continuously strive to reduce our material footprint and environmental impact. Most of our packaging is made from recyclable materials, ensuring your blooms arrive in the best condition while caring for the planet.

To learn more about us, our products, and our methods, please reach out at hello@theflora.in. Thank you for choosing The Flora!
