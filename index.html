About The FloraNest

The FloraNest (legally registered as Floradesign India Pvt. Ltd.) is a flower company offering online delivery across India. Customers can choose from an exquisite selection of flower bouquets and arrangements crafted by our expert florists. We are passionate about sustainability and grow our flowers on a farm that practices zero-waste methods.

Our eco-friendly approach means we constantly work to reduce our footprint. Most of our packaging is recyclable, ensuring your blooms arrive fresh while caring for the environment.
